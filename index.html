The word “question” does not exist without the word “quest” inside it.

I’ve designed 100 questions (100Qs) that organize a story of your life.

Each day, you answer only 1 question… for less than 100 seconds.
You don’t need any fancy equipment. Just your phone camera.

By the end of 100 days, you will have written the autobiography of your life — transforming your raw experiences, deepest struggles, intimate moments, natural talents, and hard-won victories into a story you fully own. Your digital legacy. The same framework I used to build my movie.

Your story is your value.

When we exist digitally, our stories transcend time and live on — helping others long after we’re gone.

Adventures in Personal Transformation

After years of working on films like The Dark Knight Rises, Interstellar, and Captain America: Civil War, I turned the camera on myself and made the most personal film of my life. Future Vibrant Self is the framework that helped me navigate divorce, physical change, grief, and unhealed trauma — and emerge freer, stronger, and more purpose-driven. This is the same path I’m now opening for others who want to author their story and sculpt into their vibrant self.
